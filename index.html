Sqube

Version 1.0

Sqube is a simple platformer game based on a square cube (Sqube) who goes through different dangerous worlds and adventures. Sqube is a relaxing game without any pressure or ads, just a fun game to play in your spare time. This version of Sqube(1.0) is the very first version of Sqube, and it is still very much in it's testing stages, but feel free to try it out and have some fun!
